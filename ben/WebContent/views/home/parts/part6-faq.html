Freelancers: While inexpensive, this strategy requires you to act as the CTO while managing isolated freelancers. This is very difficult to do well. Large Development Firms: These larger development firms typically complete projects for the Fortune 500, so you can expect costs to be several hundred thousand dollars. If you have the funds, this is a feasible alternative, but we think working with Uhray could save you A LOT of time and money.
